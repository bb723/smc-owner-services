Standard Management Company is a local, in-house property management team serving owners across central Maine. We market your units, screen your tenants, write your leases, take the tenant calls, and keep your maintenance scheduled, so owning rentals feels less like a second job. Here is exactly what we handle.

What we do for you

Included

Marketing & advertising

We build the listing and publish it to multiple sites, including Facebook Marketplace and Craigslist. Most of the time this is free; when housing demand is soft we may suggest a small ad budget to move the unit faster.

Included

Record keeping & rent reconciliation

You get a clean owner statement every month, with rent reconciled into your books. We also send late-rent reminders and notices at your request.

Included

Tenant prequalification

We pre-screen every applicant on stated credit and background before anyone sees the unit, and we line up the most qualified prospects to view it first.

Included

Showings & unit filling

Once your lockbox is up, we schedule self-guided showings from our list of prequalified applicants and field their questions, so the unit gets seen without you driving over.

Included

Tenant screening

When we have a strong applicant, we verify everything with a full background and credit check through AppFolio before you commit.

Included

Electronic lease writing

We draft and execute every lease through DocuSign, using leases that conform to Maine state standards. No paperwork on your end.

Included

24/7 tenant calls

We answer your tenants around the clock and handle the conversation, so you do not have to take the call. You stay informed, not interrupted.

Included

Routine maintenance scheduling

We coordinate the recurring work: HVAC maintenance, lawns mowed, snow removed, so nothing slips through the cracks.

Conditional

After-hours emergencies

Calls between 5:00 PM and 8:00 AM are handled at our after-hours rate. If a vendor performs the work, the vendor's after-hours rate applies.

Ad hoc

Cleaning

We arrange cleaning for your units as needed, billed per agreement with our cleaning partners.

How it works with us

You keep your rent

Your rent stays in your account. You pay us once at the end of each month. We never hold your money.

We pay your bills

We pay your property's bills on your behalf through AppFolio, so the operating side runs without you chasing it.

We are local and in-house

Our own crew is on the ground in central Maine. We coordinate the work and stand behind it, not a faceless call center.

Preventative maintenance & seasonal care

Good management is not just filling units, it is keeping them healthy so the small things never become big bills. We schedule the following for you by default so they never get forgotten. They are billed at our standard rates, and you can opt out anytime if you would rather handle them yourself.

By default

Annual preventative maintenance

Once a year we clean your dryer vents, flush the water heater, clear the bathroom exhaust vents, and clean the coils behind the refrigerators. The small stuff that quietly prevents fires, system failures, and surprise repair bills. Billed at our standard hourly labor rate.

By default

Seasonal lawn cleanups

Spring and fall yard cleanups keep the property looking cared for and protect it from leaf buildup, clogged gutters, and freeze damage heading into a Maine winter. Billed at our lawn-care rate.

Scheduled by default and billed at our standard rates. Prefer to handle them yourself? Just say the word and we will leave them off.
